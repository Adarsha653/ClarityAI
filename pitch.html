Key Features
🧠

Context-Aware AI

Knows your stress score, situation, triggers, and session history. Gives specific advice, not generic tips.

🪞

CBT Coaching

Challenges self-doubt thoughts like "What if they realize I don't know enough?" using cognitive reframing.

📈

Measurable Impact

Before/after stress scores per session. Real data showing your recovery, not just streaks.

🏷️

Workplace Stressors

Select specific triggers: Deadline Pressure, Imposter Syndrome, Manager Conflict, and more.

🎙️

Voice Chat

Speak to the AI and hear responses. Like talking to a real coach, in English or Nepali.

🔒

Private & Secure

Bcrypt-hashed passwords, JWT auth. Your conversations are not stored permanently.
